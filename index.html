Our Story

Born from a passion
for refined beauty.

Kayaal Beauty Lounge was born from a singular vision to bring a truly elevated beauty experience to Kodakara. In a town where beauty is deeply personal and community-rooted, Kayaal exists as a quiet sanctuary: somewhere between a luxury destination and a trusted home.

Every detail - the black and white interiors, the curated treatments, the unhurried pace - is a deliberate choice to make you feel exceptional. Not because you need to change, but because you deserve to feel exactly as extraordinary as you are.

Najeeb Shahul
Founder, Kayaal Beauty Lounge
